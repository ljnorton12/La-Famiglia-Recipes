Start Baking!

  • 1 pie dough
  • Vegetable oil spray
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup (1 3/4 oz) plus 1 tbs sugar, measured seperate
  • 1 pound peaches, pitted and cut into 1/2 inch wedges
  • 1 cup (5 oz) blackberries
  • All-purpose flour (for sprinkling on counter)
  • 1 tbsp (1/2 oz) water

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees and adjust to be placed center rack.
  2. Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper and lightly spray with vegetable oil spray.
  3. In large bowl, whisk cornstarch and 1/4 cup sugar until well combined. Add peaches and blackberries stirring with spatula until coated.
  4. Sprinkle little flower over clean counter. Place dough on floured counter and sprinkle dough with a little extra flower.
  5. Use rolling pin to roll dough into 12-inch circle, rotating dough and reflowering counter inbetween rolls.
  6. Use your hands to gently transfer dough to parchment-lined baking sheet.
  7. Use rubber spatula to scrape fruit mixture into center of dough. Spread into even layer, leaving 2-inch border around edges.
  8. Fold 2-inch border of dough up and over edge of filling. Continue folding, overlapping folds every 2 inches around the tart.
  9. Use pastry brush to lightly paint dough with water. Sprinkle dough and fruit with remaining 1 tbs sugar.
  10. Place baking sheet in oven (Center rack!) and bake 45-50 minutes. Or until dough is golden brown and fruit is bubbling.
  11. Once done, place baking sheet on cooling rack for 30 minutes.
  12. Serve warm or at room temperature.
